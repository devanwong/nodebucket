What is NodeBucket?

NodeBucket is a simple application to help our clients stay organized. We know how sometimes we forget our post-its at home, or how difficult it is to read your own or someone else’s handwriting, and even sometimes the ‘notes’ on our smart phone can be pure chaos. That is why NodeBucket was created to help create and manage tasks easy and simple. It has a simple and responsive interface, which is compatible for all devices. Even your homeschooled child during COVID times can use it.
We hope you enjoy it.

To Get Started:

Simply, press create task.
Type in your todo task like ‘take out garbage’.
When you are done with your task, go ahead and drag it to the done.
When you have made an error or want to remove it, you can click on the three dots and press remove.

Version 2.0:
